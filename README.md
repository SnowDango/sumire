## About

Sumire fetches the songs you play on music services and saves them as a history.
You can browse the song you are currently listening to as well as your past playback history.
A widget is also available, allowing you to view the currently playing song, copy its URL, and share it to X with ease.

## Requirements

- **Android 13 (API 33) or later**

## Supported Music Services

### Playback Detection

Music apps whose playback can be captured from notifications:

- Apple Music

### URL Retrieval & Sharing

URLs of the detected song can be retrieved and shared on the following platforms:

- Apple Music
- Spotify
- YouTube Music
- Google
- Amazon Music
- Deezer
- Tidal
- Pandora
- Napster
- Yandex

## Usage

Download the latest APK from the [Releases](https://github.com/SnowDango/sumire/releases) page and install it.
After installation, launch the app and grant the permissions it requests when prompted.

### Required Permissions

- **Notification access** — Used to detect the currently playing song. The app cannot function without this permission.
- **Notification display** — Optional. Without it, widget error notifications will not be shown.
